About ForgeLinks

We bridge the gap between AI and the construction industry — built by people who understand both worlds.

The Problem We Saw

Construction is a trillion-dollar industry running on email threads, paper forms, and spreadsheets. 60% of work time is spent on "work about work" — chasing documents, synthesizing scattered information, tracking deadlines, and assembling reports that should have been done yesterday.

Small contractors are losing thousands in unbilled change orders and overdue invoices. PMs are drowning in admin instead of managing projects. Office managers are buried in compliance paperwork. And the critical information flowing between field and office gets lost in text threads and voicemails.

Generic AI tools don't solve this. They don't understand construction workflows, document formats, or industry terminology. They produce chat responses when you need structured, reviewable documents.

We built something that does.

// The old way
1. Dig through email threads
2. Cross-reference field reports
3. Check meeting notes & specs
4. Reconcile quotes & change orders
5. Draft documents manually
6. Track deadlines in spreadsheets
7. Chase follow-ups one by one
= hours per day, every day

// The ForgeLinks way
1. Drop files in
2. Review structured output
3. Approve and go
= 30 seconds per task

What We Believe

Our principles shape every agent we build.

🏗️

Built for Construction

Not adapted from generic tools. Our agents understand RFIs, change orders, SWPPP inspections, spec sections, grid references, and the way real jobsites communicate. Domain specificity is our advantage.

🧑‍💼

Human Decision-Making

AI does the synthesis, formatting, and cross-referencing. Humans make the decisions. Every output is reviewed before it leaves the office. We don't automate judgment — we free it up.

💡

Transparent Process

Our split-view review UI shows you exactly what went in and what came out. No black boxes. You see the source emails, the agent's reasoning, and the final documents — side by side.

⚡

Speed Without Shortcuts

Fast doesn't mean sloppy. Our agents produce documents with proper drawing references, accurate math, correct spec citations, and complete audit trails — in seconds, not hours.

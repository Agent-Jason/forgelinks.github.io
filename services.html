Our Services

Six service areas built around the workflows that cost construction teams the most time, money, and headaches.

Service 01

Project Administration

Construction project managers spend hours every day on documentation — chasing RFIs, assembling daily logs, writing meeting minutes, tracking submittals, and backing up change orders. Our agent handles the document-heavy admin so PMs can manage the actual project.

  • Ingest emails, field reports, photos, meeting notes, and coordination drawings
  • Classify and route items — RFI, submittal, change order, daily log, site issue
  • Draft the next artifact with proper references and formatting
  • Extract deadlines, action items, and dependencies automatically
  • Maintain a live project action register and clean audit trail
  • Flag missing documentation, verbal approvals, and schedule risks

Service 02

Billing & Cash Flow Operations

Over half of small businesses are owed money from unpaid invoices — averaging $17.5K each. Our agent handles the entire invoice-to-cash pipeline: reconciling quotes against completed work, generating invoices, following up on overdue balances, and keeping your AR visible.

  • Reconcile original quotes against actual work and change orders
  • Generate accurate invoices with line items, adjustments, and payment terms
  • Draft customer-specific send emails with invoice details
  • Produce tiered payment reminders based on days overdue
  • Flag risky accounts, verbal-only approvals, and missing documentation
  • Build AR dashboards with aging buckets and collection priorities

Field-to-Office Coordination

Critical jobsite information arrives as text messages, emailed photos, voicemails, and handwritten notes. By the time it reaches the office, details are scattered and context is lost. Our agent bridges the gap — turning raw field communications into structured, actionable reports.

  • Ingest field texts, photos, voice memos, and email updates
  • Consolidate scattered communications by project and topic
  • Assemble daily field reports from raw inputs
  • Extract issues, decisions, and site conditions with timestamps
  • Route urgent items to the right person with full context
  • Create a searchable record of field communications

Schedule & Deadline Operations

  • Generate daily priority dashboards for PMs and superintendents
  • Automate follow-up reminders with escalation tiers
  • Flag likely schedule slips before they become critical

Reporting & Visibility

Getting a clear picture of project status, outstanding issues, and financial health usually means pulling data from five different systems. Our agent assembles the picture for you — from your existing data, with no new platforms to adopt.

  • Produce financial roll-ups: billed vs. budgeted, outstanding AR, cost-to-complete

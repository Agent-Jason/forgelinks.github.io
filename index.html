The Problems Burying Construction Teams

These aren't edge cases. They're the daily reality for every GC, subcontractor, and trades firm in the country. Each one is a workflow our agents can handle.

📁

Project Admin Overload

RFIs, submittals, daily logs, meeting minutes, change order backup, drawing lookups — PMs spend hours a day on documentation instead of managing the project. One missed item can trigger delays and disputes.

💸

Cash Flow Gaps

56% of small businesses are owed money from unpaid invoices. Invoices go out late, change orders get lost, follow-ups don't happen, and AR ages while the owner chases payments manually.

📋

Compliance & Records Chaos

Licenses, insurance certificates, permits, safety paperwork, payroll records — all with different expiration dates. One missed renewal can shut down a jobsite or void your coverage.

📧

Field-to-Office Coordination

Critical information lives in text threads, voicemails, emailed photos, and handwritten notes. By the time it reaches the office, details are scattered, and context is lost.

⏰

Deadline & Follow-Up Tracking

Action items from meetings, submittal due dates, inspection schedules, owner response deadlines — tracked in spreadsheets, sticky notes, or not tracked at all. Things slip.

✈️

Travel & Logistics

Site visits, inspections, vendor meetings across multiple projects — your team is constantly traveling. Coordinating schedules, booking flights, and updating calendars eats up hours that should go toward the work itself.
